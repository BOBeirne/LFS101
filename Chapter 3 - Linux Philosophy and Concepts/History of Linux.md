
- [[Linux]] is and Open-Source OS 
- It was developed on and for Intel x86 based PCs
- It support's now a wide-variety of hardware


## 1991

Linus Torvalds started writing his own Kernel in 1991 in Helsinki
## 1992

In 1992 Linus re-licensed Linux using [[GPL]] by GNU, which allowed it to create a world-wide community of developers

Combining Kernel with other OS components from GNU project to created a first Linux distributions (mid 90s) 

## 1998

Major companies like IBM and Oracle announced official Linux support which drove major development efforts

## Back in the day

- Installing Linux used to be very time-consuming requiring downloading data over phone lines onto floppy disks and loading them one-by-one
- eventually installations got upgraded into CD and DVD format
## Today

Linux powers:
- more than 96% of web servers 
- majority of smartphones
- All most powerful supercomputers
