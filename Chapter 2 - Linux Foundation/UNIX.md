- **Development** started: 1969 in [Bell Labs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_Labs), former Subsidiary of [AT&T](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AT%26T_Corporation)
- **1st** **released** (internally) in Nov 1971
- **2nd released** (public) 1973
- AT&T **sold** Unix to [Novell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Novell "Novell") in 1992, which **then sold it to** [the Open Group](https://www.opengroup.org/unix-systems)


## System

- Written in C
- Allowed it tobe portable and operate on numerous platforms
- Characterized by modular design, aka "[[Unix philosophy]]"
